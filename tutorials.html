EcoWatt - Renewable Energy Sources

Solar Energy

Solar energy is harnessed from the sun's rays and converted into electricity or heat.

Wind Energy

Wind energy is generated by wind turbines, which convert the kinetic energy of wind into electricity.

Hydroelectric Power

Hydroelectric power is produced by capturing the energy from flowing water.

Geothermal Energy

Geothermal energy is harnessed by tapping into the heat stored beneath the Earth's surface.
